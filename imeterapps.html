iMeter Apps for IN/IM/IS Series Mailing Systems

Postal Rates

The Postal Rates App provides automatic delivery of updates when the Postal Service™ announces a change to its rates, delivery service fees and postal zones. Updates are downloaded quickly through the IM-Series Smart Connect™ high speed LAN connection, and ensure accurate rating of the exact postage for all of your items.
Standard on all IM/IS Series Systems - Never pay for a rate change again!


Postal Expense Manager

The Online Postal Expense Manager™ App provides web reporting on postage usage and trends by department, time period, and postal class, which helps with budget planning. Reports can be viewed as graphical snapshots, and all postage usage data can be easily exported into Excel® for additional analysis and reporting.
Optional


E-Services

Experience significant cost savings and simple processing and tracking of your mail with the Hasler E-Services App. User definable email alerts inform you of delivered and undelivered mail. This allows you to inform your recipient of the delivery status, which can help build stronger relationships with your customers. With the E-Services App, you will be able to utilize discounted postal rates for USPS® Extra Services including eDelivery Confirmation™, eSignature Confirmation™, and tracking of eCertfied Mail™.

TotalFunds

The TotalFunds® App provides the flexibility and convenience of funding your iMeter™ with a "download now, pay later" TotalFunds® postage financing plan.
