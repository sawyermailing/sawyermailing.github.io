Our Promise

At Sawyer Mailing Systems, when you call for service, you can expect a phone call from a Factory Trained Service Technician within 1 hour. Many times we can troubleshoot and resolve problems over the phone, saving you valuable time. If we cannot resolve your problem over the phone, we promise an industry best 4 hour on-site response time. If we are unable to restore full functionality to your equipment within 24 hours of our original arrival, we will furnish your organization with a loaner.

Responsibility

We are as committed to the environment as we are to our customers. All obsolete equipment and supplies are recycled.

Factory Parts

Rest assured, when our technicians service your equipment, only OEM replacement parts are being used. This maximizes the efficiency as well as the life of your equipment.
